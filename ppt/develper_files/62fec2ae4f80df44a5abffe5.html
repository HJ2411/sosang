Latest Updates

newAdded support for Arabic, Farsi, Hebrew, and Urdu
We’re excited to announce that Gamma now supports right to left (RTL) languages Arabic, Farsi, Hebrew, and Urdu. These are currently in beta, so please submit your feedback on them here .
newIntroducing social content
Gamma now offers social formats in addition to standard presentations and documents! This update allows you to create content optimized for social media, making it easier to share your ideas in visually engaging, platform-friendly layouts. Social content is now accessible to all users in the new from blank flow on the Gamma dashboard, as well as page setup inside the Gamma. Stay tuned for social content inside the AI generator - coming soon!
newScale your content to fit!
We’re thrilled to introduce a new feature in Gamma that lets you scale your content to fit fixed-size formats like 16:9 presentations. With this update, you no longer need to manually adjust overflowing content—Gamma now allows you to automatically resize your content to fit neatly within the card boundaries.
